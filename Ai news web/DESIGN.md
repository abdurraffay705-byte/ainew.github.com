# Design System: Simple AI News Hub
**Project ID:** 9214832737885625344

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design philosophy is **Clean Accessibility**. It focuses on high legibility and a professional "Tech-Journal" aesthetic. The interface is light and airy, using generous whitespace to reduce cognitive load, making it easy for anyone to browse daily AI news.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Crisp White (#ffffff):** Primary background color for all screens.
* **Royal Blue (#2463eb):** Primary accent color for buttons, active navigation states, and key highlights like badges.
* **Charcoal (#1f2937):** Primary text color for headlines and important UI elements to ensure high contrast.
* **Slate Gray (#4b5563):** Secondary text color for article snippets, meta-data (author, date), and descriptions.
* **Soft Light Gray (#f9fafb):** Used for sidebar backgrounds or subtle search bar fills.
* **Subtle Border Gray (#e5e7eb):** Used for card borders and section dividers.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** **Work Sans** (A versatile, highly legible geometric sans-serif).
* **Headings:** Bold weights (700), Charcoal (#1f2937).
* **Body:** Regular (400) or Medium (500) weights, Slate Gray (#4b5563), with a comfortable line-height (1.6) for extended reading.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 8px rounded corners (ROUND_EIGHT), Royal Blue (#2463eb) background with white text. Subtly expansive hover states.
* **Cards:** Minimal 1px border (#e5e7eb), 8px rounded corners, and a whisper-soft diffused shadow on hover for depth.
* **Search Bar:** Pill-shaped (rounded-full) or 8px rounded, centered, with a light gray fill and a subtle stroke.
* **Badges:** Small, high-contrast labels (e.g., "5 min read") using Royal Blue background or borders.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Grid:** Simple vertical list for news feeds on mobile, masonry or multi-column grid on desktop.
* **Margins:** Large, consistent margins (16px to 32px) to provide breathability.
* **Hierarchy:** Clear distinction between hero content and secondary news feed using scale and weight.
